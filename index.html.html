Subscription-Based Ad Viewing & Advertising Platform

Project Overview

A comprehensive mobile-first digital advertising platform that revolutionizes how users interact with advertisements. The platform creates a unique ecosystem where users can subscribe to view approved advertisements, earn rewards through engagement and referrals, publish their own ads as advertisers, and withdraw earnings to their bank accounts.

Earn While You Watch

Users earn reward points for every verified ad view, which can be converted to real money and withdrawn to their bank account.

Advertise Your Business

Publish and manage your own advertisements with detailed analytics and moderation support to reach your target audience.

Referral Rewards

Earn 6% on each referral subscription plus 100% subscription refund after achieving 15 verified referrals.

Income Statements

Download monthly and yearly income statements for record-keeping and compliance purposes with complete earnings breakdown.

Application Concept

Dual-Role System

Each user can seamlessly switch between two roles within the platform:

Buyer (Ad Viewer) Access advertisements through subscription plans, earn rewards for engagement, and refer friends for additional bonuses.
Seller (Advertiser) Publish and manage advertisements, track performance analytics, and reach target audiences effectively.

Key Features

Secure Registration OTP-based mobile authentication with ID verification for sellers to ensure platform security.
Content Moderation All advertisements are reviewed and approved before publication to ensure quality and appropriateness.
Trending Algorithm Engagement metrics determine advertisement visibility and trending status based on user interaction.
Bank Integration Direct withdrawal of earnings to linked bank accounts with secure transaction processing.

Referral & Incentive System

How It Works

Every user receives a unique referral code upon registration. When new users sign up using this code and purchase a subscription, the referrer earns rewards.

Per-Referral Reward Earn 6% of each referred user's subscription amount, instantly credited to your wallet.
Milestone Bonus After 15 verified referrals, receive 100% refund of your original subscription amount.
Validation Rules Referrals count only when the new user completes registration AND purchases a subscription.
Withdrawable All referral earnings can be withdrawn to your bank account along with other earnings.
